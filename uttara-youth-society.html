Who We Are

Building a Stronger Community, Together

Uttara Youth Society is a non-profit social organization dedicated to empowering young people in Uttara, Dhaka — through education, community service, and leadership development.

Our Mission: To nurture responsible, compassionate, and active citizens who contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Vision: A thriving Uttara community where every young person has the opportunity to lead and grow.

Founded: Established in Uttara, Dhaka with a commitment to volunteerism and social impact.

Our Values: Unity, Integrity, Inclusivity, and Continuous Learning guide everything we do.

What We Do

Our Programs & Activities

From community clean-ups to leadership workshops — our programs are designed to create real impact.

📚

Education Support

Free tutoring, study circles, and scholarship awareness campaigns for underprivileged students in the Uttara area.

🌱

Environment Drives

Tree plantations, neighborhood clean-up campaigns, and awareness drives to protect our local environment.

🩺

Health Camps

Free medical check-ups, blood donation events, and health awareness programs for the community.

🎯

Leadership Training

Workshops, seminars, and mentorship programs to develop the next generation of community leaders.

🎨

Cultural Events

Celebrating national days, cultural festivals, and creative expression through arts and performance.

🤝

Social Welfare

Winter clothing drives, food distribution, and support for families in need during emergencies.
